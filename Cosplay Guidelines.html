Beecon 2025 Cosplay Guidelines!


1.Q : If we pay the registration fee, do we still have to buy a ticket?
A : No, for contestants, your registration fee is equivalent to your entry ticket on Day 2 only (February 16). Assistants must purchase the regular tickets.

2.Q : Age limit for the Cosplay competition.
A : We have agreed to have the age limit of at least 15 years old and above both for Solo and Group Cosplay Category.

3.Q : Clarification of the costumes worn that have won cosplay contests.
A : For example, if you have cosplayed LUFFY in any cosplay competition and you WON as CHAMPION/FIRST PLACE, you are NO LONGER allowed to use that costume in this contest. This is to promote diversity and creativity. BUT, if you used a costume on a cosplay contest and ranked second place and below, you may use it in this event. We are referring only to the costume and not the cosplayer themselves.

4.Q : Are we allowed to crossplay or genderbend?
A : Yes! You can cosplay any character you like regardless of gender as long as it will not violate any rules and mechanics of this contest.

5.Q : Is the cosplay character limited to gaming?
A : No. You can cosplay any anime, cartoon, series, movie, video or mobile game characters etc. This is not limited to game characters.

6.Q : Can we use youtube links for our audio-video background?
A : You may utilize a youtube video as your audio-video (AV) background of your cosplay performance but you should upload the AV file
in Google drive and attach the link to your registration form or you may message Mugiwaras of Negros to send the copy of the AV file.
*Disclaimer : The organizers will not be held liable for any copyright infringements.

7.Q : When are we going to pay the registration fees?
A : Mugiwaras of Negros will make an announcement and contact all registered participants regarding payment of registration fees.

8.Q : Are we going to print our own reference pictures?
A : No need. Mugiwaras of Negros will print it for you.

9.Q : In the group cosplay category, should all characters be in the same anime/game/series/movies etc.?
A : No, you are free to decide which characters you will cosplay as a group and it is up to you on
how you will connect them all in your group performance.

10. Q: until when is the registration?
A : Until all slots are full
