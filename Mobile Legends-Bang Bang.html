Beecon 2025 MLBB Guidelines! 

  
Tournament Overview
● Tournament Name: Beecon 2024 - MLBB Tournament
● Organizer: Higala League
● Game: MLBB
● Platform: Mobile 

● Age Requirement: No age limit; however, minors must secure a parental consent form
to participate.
● Account Level: MLBB accounts must be at least Legend rank.
● Region: OPEN TO ALL
● Team Registration: Teams must register with a roster of 5 players + 1 substitute if
available.
 
 



   

       









    Registration  
● All teams must register through Google Forms which will be posted on the official Facebook page of BEECON 2025
● Registration Deadline: January 15, 2025 (PASSED)
○ Each team must consist of 5 players and may have up to 1 substitute player.
● Participation Fee: Each participating player is required to pay a fee of 250 pesos. This
fee includes a one-day pass for access to the tournament area. Only teams that qualify
for the finals will receive free access on the second day.
● Discord Requirement: All players must join the tournament Discord server at this link
for communication and updates.
● Registration Requirements
○ Each team must submit a full roster with their designated captain.
○ Players must provide in-game IDs and a valid form of contact for updates.
○ All registered players must use their own MLBB accounts; no sharing accounts is
allowed.

   Match Rules  
● Match Lobby Setup: Custom-Draft Lobby
● Map: Official MLBB map
● Victory Conditions: The team that destroys the enemy base first wins.
● Side Selection:
○ General Side Selection (Elimination to Semi-Finals): Standard Coin toss Pickings for Blue side.
○ Grand Finals Side Selection: The starting side for Game 1 will be determined
by a coin toss, with the winning team choosing the blue or red side. Teams will
then alternate sides from Game 2 to Game 4. If the series reaches Game 5,
another coin toss will determine the side selection.


● Game Settings:
○ Hero Ban and Pick Phase: Standard competitive draft. Each team will have a
specified time limit to ban and pick heroes. 



● Allowed Features:
○ Stickers and recalls are allowed.
○ Quick chats are allowed.
○ All chat is prohibited.

● Pauses:
○ Elimination to Semi-Finals: Pauses are not permitted in elimination to
semi-final matches. Pauses are allowed only in the finals.
○ Technical Pauses (Grand Finals Only): Each team is allowed one technical
pause per match, with a maximum duration of 2 minutes.
○ Pause Protocol: All pauses must be clearly communicated to officials.

● Disconnection Policy: 
○ Players who disconnect must attempt to reconnect. If reconnecting is not
possible, the match continues unless officials declare a rematch due to severe
disruption.
○ In the event of a disconnection, players must rejoin the game as quickly as
possible.

-Match Conduct-
● Player Requirements: All players must be present and ready within 10 minutes of the
scheduled match time.
 

Broadcasting and Media 
● Streaming: Only selected matches will be broadcast live at the venue. Players are
strictly prohibited from streaming the tournament themselves.
● Recording: Players consent to being recorded and have their gameplay used for
promotional content.




Final Decisions 
● All final decisions rest with the tournament organizers. Organizers reserve the right to
modify rules as necessary.
● All decisions regarding the interpretation of these rules from the tournament organizer
will be final.
● Change of rules may be amended, modified, or supplemented by the tournament
organizer from time to time, to ensure fair play and the integrity of the tournament
