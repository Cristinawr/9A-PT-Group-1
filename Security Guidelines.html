Security Guidelines!

1.Q : What are the prohibited objects to bring?
A : Sharp objects, Pyro Materials, Smoke Materials and Hazardous Materials are strictly Prohibited.

2.Q : Age limit for Con-goers.
A : No Age limit for Con-goers, but make sure to supervise Minors or People below the age of 18.

3.Q : How do we apply to become Security or Volunteers?.
A : We have our own security agency and set of volunteers ready for the event. The application process has been CLOSED.

4.Q : How do we enter the Convention?
A : We are holding BeeCon at Funtion Halls 1-3. You may enter through the 2 entrances on the 3rd floor. Entrance through ingress is strictly prohibited.

5.Q : What can we sell?
A : Unless you are a Concessionaire, Artist, Merchant or Community Partner, You are prohibited from selling any products to the public.

6.Q : Do you have a dresscode?
A : No, Since there will be cosplayers, players and ordinary people present, there will be no enforced dresscode.

7.Q : What happens if we violate any of the security guidelines?
A : BeeCon will make an announcement and contact any nearby security or staff to escort the violator outside the premises
of the convention.

8.Q If people bought an item in the merchants, will refunds be acknowledged?
A : If transactions escalate into commotion, security and staff will be called to handle the situation based
on the Rules of Engagement.

9.Q Is there a limit to how many can attend the con?
A : No, theres no limitations to the amount of people that may attend the convention.

10.Q : How do we identify Staff & Security?
A : No need. Beecon 2025 will print out Lanyards and ID's for them to be easily identified when needed.

11.Q : Are there any limitations regarding # of Objects/Props brought?
A : No, you may bring as many Objects/Props as you want as long as they are not hazardous.

12. Q: until when is the Convention for?
A : 10am-8pm, People staying inside the convention center after closing time will be escorted out.
