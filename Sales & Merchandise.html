Sales & Merchandise Guidelines!

1.Q : If we pay the registration fee & we are Concessionaire Merchants, Artists or Community Partners, do we still have to buy a ticket?
A : No, for your category, your registration fee is equivalent to your entry ticket on Day 1&2. Assistants must purchase the regular tickets.

2.Q : Age limit for Concessionaire/Merchants/Artists.
A : We have agreed to have the age limit of at least 18 years old and above to be able to become a Concessionaire/Merchant/Artist.

3.Q : How do we apply to become Community Partners?.
A : If you own a company, organization, community or group, we will reach out to YOU. This is due to the influx of applicants for community partners,
we will reach out to those who we deem may apply for a Community Partner Booth and Access.

4.Q : How do we apply to become Merchants/Concessionaire/Artist?
A : You can apply for one of these positions and acquire a booth by messaging us thru the Official BeeCon 2025 FB Page.

5.Q : What can we sell?
A : For Concessionaires, Food & Drink Products. For Merchants & Artists, Any Merchandise & Art
such as Pyro, Smoke or other hazardous materials is prohibited. The rest is allowed.

6.Q : How big will our booths be?
A : The booth that will be given to you is depending on your placement inside the convention center.
Regular Booth - 3sqm. Premium booth - 5sqm. Community Partner - 8 sqm.

7.Q : When are we going to pay the registration fees?
A : BeeCon will make an announcement and contact all registered booth holders regarding payment of registration fees.

8.Q If people bought an item in the merchants, will refunds be acknowledged?
A : It depends on the merchant you bought your item from but if you bought an item from the official beecon store you could refund it if it has an issue.

9.Q Is there a limit to how much the cost of the sold product?
A : No, theres no limitations to the cost of the product you are selling so you could sell valuable stuff such as limited expensive items.

10.Q : Are we going to print our own Lanyards, IDs & Booth Names?
A : No need. Beecon 2025 will print it for you.

11.Q : Are there any limitations regarding # of staff/assistants?
A : No, you may bring as many staff/assistants as you want.

12. Q: until when is the registration?
A : Until all slots are full

13. Q: Can we resell Tickets?
A : No, reselling of Beecon Tickets is strictly prohibited.

14. Q: When do we set-up?
A : For Concessionaires, 6am of Feb. 15 | For Merchants, Artists & Community Partners, You may set-up as early as 4pm of Feb. 14
